ABOUT

Project Mura

A VR platform for nonverbal expression, reflective healing, and personalized emotional care. It helps users move from internal emotion recognition to guided engagement in a responsive, stigma-free environment.

ABOUT

Why It Matters

Designed for schools and wellness programs, Mura supports emotional literacy and private reflection through calm spaces, guided meditations, and an optional companion mode for supportive AI dialogue.

FEATURES

Three Modes, One Journey

Mura offers three distinct paths for emotional healing — Stillness, Meditation, and Companion — allowing users to explore in their own way, at their own pace.

01 Stillness Mode

Enter a quiet, ambient environment designed for reflection and calm. Stillness Mode provides space to breathe and reset, free from guidance or dialogue.

SilenceFocusCalm

02 Meditation Mode

Guided meditation sessions blend soothing narration, soundscapes, and visuals. Each experience adapts to your emotional check-in, helping you find balance and clarity.

NarratedAdaptiveMindful

03 Companion Mode

A conversational AI companion listens, reflects, and responds with empathy. It offers gentle prompts to explore emotions, fostering understanding without judgment.

AI DialogueEmpathyReflection

HOW IT WORKS

Your Journey with Mura

From the first emotional check-in to reflective closure — each stage unfolds naturally, guiding you through self-awareness and calm.

01

Emotional Check-In

Begin by identifying your current feeling or intention. This moment of awareness sets the emotional tone for your experience.

02

Select Your Mode

Choose Stillness, Meditation, or Companion mode — each tailored to your comfort and emotional needs.

03

Immerse in the Space

Enter a responsive VR environment that mirrors your emotions with sound, light, and gentle ambient motion.

04

Engage & Reflect

Practice mindfulness or talk with the AI companion. Every action deepens your emotional clarity and understanding.

05

Save Your Moment

Conclude with a reflection or simply take your calm with you. Mura saves your journey, ready for your next session.
